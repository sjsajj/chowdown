ingredients:
- 1 jar of organic tart cherry juice
- 2.5 cups of fresh lime juice* (10-15 limes)
- Equal parts water to juice
- 2.5 cups of sugar
- Lime wedges for garnish

directions:
- Combine all ingredients
- Chill
- Enjoy
